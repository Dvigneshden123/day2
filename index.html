chhatrapati shivaji maharaj

In honour of the founder of the Maratha Kingdom, Shivaji Jayanti is celebrated every year on February 19. Shivaji Maharaj was born on March 21, 1690, in Pune Shivneri fort. It is the 393rd birth anniversary of the mighty Maratha this year. Shivaji Jayanti is primarily a Maharashtrian festival celebrated as a holiday.
